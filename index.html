About

Hi, I'm Eric!

I'm a UX Designer that enjoys solving difficult problems by applying human-centered design techniques. I've been designing for more than 20 years.

I'm driven by building relationships with people and teams. I believe that empathy is the key to relationship building. It's been my experience that building great relationships has led to creating exceptional products.

The user is invariably at the core of my solutions. I continuously keep in mind that I am not the user. I've been successful by testing with real users and always humbly seeking improvement.
